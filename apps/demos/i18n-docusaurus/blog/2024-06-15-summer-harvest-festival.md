Summer is in full swing at Metro Gardens! Join us for our annual Summer Harvest Festival as we celebrate the incredible bounty that our community gardeners have cultivated.

<!-- truncate -->

## Festival Highlights

This year's festival promises to be our best yet, with activities for gardeners of all ages and experience levels.

### What to Expect

- **Garden Tours**: Guided tours of our most productive plots
- **Cooking Demos**: Live cooking demonstrations using fresh garden produce
- **Kids Activities**: Scavenger hunts and planting activities for young gardeners
- **Seed Swap**: Exchange seeds with fellow gardeners
- **Potluck Dinner**: Share dishes made with your homegrown ingredients

### Schedule

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 10:00 AM | Gates open, garden tours begin |
| 11:00 AM | Cooking demonstration |
| 12:00 PM | Kids activities |
| 2:00 PM | Seed swap |
| 5:00 PM | Community potluck |

## This Season's Stars

Our gardeners have had exceptional success with several crops this year:

- **Tomatoes**: Record yields from our heirloom varieties
- **Peppers**: Beautiful bell peppers and spicy varieties
- **Squash**: Abundant zucchini and summer squash
- **Herbs**: Thriving basil, rosemary, and thyme

## Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just curious about community gardening, everyone is welcome at our festival. It's a great opportunity to meet fellow gardeners, learn new techniques, and enjoy the fruits (and vegetables!) of our collective labor.
