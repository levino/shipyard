## Week 91 Distribution Summary

This week at Metro Community Garden, we harvested a total of **134 kg** of fresh produce from our plots. The harvest was distributed to **24 families** in our local community.

## Harvest Breakdown

- **Broccoli**: 14 kg
- **Late tomatoes**: 35 kg
- **Cauliflower**: 23 kg
- **Pears**: 11 kg

## Distribution Notes

The autumn season continues to provide excellent growing conditions. Volunteer coordinators distributed the harvest at our Saturday morning market and through our weekday pickup program.

## Community Impact

This week's distribution marks another successful week of supporting local families with fresh, organically grown produce. Our regular recipients include seniors, young families, and neighbors facing food insecurity.
