## Introduction

Welcome to another installment of our community garden guide series. Today we're exploring fall garden preparation.

Our experienced gardeners have shared their wisdom on this topic:

Community member Sarah Chen recommends starting small and scaling up as you gain confidence. "The best garden is one you can maintain," she often says.
