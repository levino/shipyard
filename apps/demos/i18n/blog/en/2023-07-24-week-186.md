## Week 186 Distribution Summary

This week at Metro Community Garden, we harvested a total of **87 kg** of fresh produce from our plots. The harvest was distributed to **39 families** in our local community.

## Harvest Breakdown

- **Melons**: 28 kg
- **Corn**: 26 kg
- **Cucumbers**: 38 kg
- **Eggplant**: 16 kg
- **Zucchini**: 13 kg

## Distribution Notes

The summer season continues to provide excellent growing conditions. Volunteer coordinators distributed the harvest at our Saturday morning market and through our weekday pickup program.

## Community Impact

This week's distribution marks another successful week of supporting local families with fresh, organically grown produce. Our regular recipients include seniors, young families, and neighbors facing food insecurity.
