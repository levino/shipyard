## Week 112 Distribution Summary

This week at Metro Community Garden, we harvested a total of **218 kg** of fresh produce from our plots. The harvest was distributed to **30 families** in our local community.

## Harvest Breakdown

- **Carrots**: 12 kg
- **Beets**: 28 kg
- **Leeks**: 22 kg
- **Turnips**: 17 kg
- **Parsnips**: 15 kg

## Distribution Notes

The winter season continues to provide excellent growing conditions. Volunteer coordinators distributed the harvest at our Saturday morning market and through our weekday pickup program.

## Community Impact

This week's distribution marks another successful week of supporting local families with fresh, organically grown produce. Our regular recipients include seniors, young families, and neighbors facing food insecurity.
