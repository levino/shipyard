## Week 166 Distribution Summary

This week at Metro Community Garden, we harvested a total of **91 kg** of fresh produce from our plots. The harvest was distributed to **17 families** in our local community.

## Harvest Breakdown

- **Peas**: 18 kg
- **Spring greens**: 32 kg
- **Lettuce**: 38 kg
- **Rhubarb**: 35 kg
- **Asparagus**: 28 kg

## Distribution Notes

The spring season continues to provide excellent growing conditions. Volunteer coordinators distributed the harvest at our Saturday morning market and through our weekday pickup program.

## Community Impact

This week's distribution marks another successful week of supporting local families with fresh, organically grown produce. Our regular recipients include seniors, young families, and neighbors facing food insecurity.
