# Q3 2024 Summary

## Accomplishments

The third quarter was focused on internationalization and expanding the component library.

## Feature Completions

- Full i18n routing support
- Language switcher component
- Fallback content system
- RTL layout support (experimental)

## Community Growth

The contributor base grew from 12 to 18 active contributors, with notable contributions from the open-source community.
