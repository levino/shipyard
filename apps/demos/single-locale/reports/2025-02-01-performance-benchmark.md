# Performance Benchmark Q4 2024

## Build Performance

Average build times across demo sites:

- **Single-locale demo**: 2.3s (down from 3.1s in Q3)
- **i18n demo**: 4.7s (down from 6.2s in Q3)
- **Versioned docs demo**: 5.1s (down from 7.8s in Q3)

## Lighthouse Scores

All demo sites consistently achieve:

- **Performance**: 98+
- **Accessibility**: 100
- **Best Practices**: 100
- **SEO**: 100

## Recommendations

Continue optimizing image handling and consider implementing incremental builds for larger sites.
