Upgrade to Astro 6. All shipyard packages now require Astro 6 (`^6.0.0`) and no longer support Astro 5. This includes updated compatibility with Zod 4, @astrojs/mdx 5, @astrojs/cloudflare 13, and @astrojs/node 10.
